Privacy Policy

We built SHIELD IQ on a simple principle: your messages are yours. We analyse them, we warn you, and then we discard them. This policy explains exactly what we do — and what we never do.

Effective Date: 1 May 2025
Last Updated: 1 May 2025
Version: 1.0
The short version — plain English
  • We never store the content of messages you scan
  • We store only scan metadata — risk band, score, timestamp, channel
  • We collect your name and email when you create an account
  • We never sell your data to anyone
  • We never share your data with third parties for marketing
  • You can delete your account and all your data at any time
  • We do not store message content — not temporarily, not in logs, not anywhere
  • We do not use your data to train AI models
01

Who We Are

SHIELD IQ is a fraud detection service that analyses messages for signs of fraud and delivers verdicts to users. SHIELD IQ is operated by SHIELD IQ Technologies (in the process of CAC registration in Nigeria).

Contact us at: dovtekofficial@gmail.com

For data protection matters specifically: dovtekofficial@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Protection".

This policy applies to all users of the SHIELD IQ web application, mobile application, and Chrome extension.

02

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service. Here is exactly what that means:

What we never collect: The content of any message you scan. When you paste a message or upload a screenshot, it is sent to our analysis engine, a verdict is generated, and the message is immediately and permanently discarded. It is never written to any database, log file, or storage system.

03

How We Use Your Data

We use your data only for the following purposes:

To provide the service — processing your scans, maintaining your account, showing your scan history, and sending account-related communications (e.g. password resets).

To improve detection accuracy — scan metadata (never message content) helps us understand what types of fraud patterns are most prevalent, which we use to improve our detection models.

To maintain security and prevent abuse — we use usage data to detect unusual patterns that may indicate abuse of the free tier or attempts to misuse the service.

To communicate with you — we may send you product updates, security notices, and account notifications. You can opt out of non-essential communications at any time.

We never use your data to train AI models. Your scan metadata is used only for service improvement within SHIELD IQ. It is never shared with or used to train any external AI system including the AI models that power our detection engine.

04

Who We Share Your Data With

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers. We do not share your data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

Service providers — we use third-party providers to host our infrastructure (e.g. cloud hosting, database services). These providers process data only on our instructions and are bound by data processing agreements. They do not have independent access to your data for their own purposes.

AI analysis providers — message content is sent to our AI analysis provider (Anthropic) solely to generate a fraud verdict. Anthropic's API processes the message and returns a result. The message is not retained by Anthropic under their API data handling terms. We recommend reviewing Anthropic's privacy policy for details.

Legal requirements — we may disclose data if required to do so by Nigerian law, court order, or other legal process. We will notify you of any such disclosure where legally permitted to do so.

Business transfer — if SHIELD IQ is acquired or merges with another entity, your data may be transferred. You will be notified in advance and given the option to delete your account before any transfer.

05

Data Storage and Security

Your account data and scan metadata are stored on secure servers. We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and encryption at rest for sensitive data including passwords.

Passwords are never stored in plain text. We use a one-way hashing algorithm (bcrypt) that means even SHIELD IQ staff cannot read your password.

Despite our best efforts, no security system is impenetrable. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal data, we will notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, in accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023.

Message content — the text you paste or the images you upload for scanning — is processed in memory only and is never written to disk or any persistent storage system at any point in the analysis pipeline.

07

Your Rights Under the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023

As a data subject under the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA), you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data and account at any time.
  • Right to Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format (JSON or CSV).
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data for specific purposes including profiling.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for any processing based on consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at dovtekofficial@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Rights Request". We will respond within 30 days. We will never charge a fee for exercising your rights.

If you are unsatisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC) at ndpc.gov.ng.

08

Children's Privacy

SHIELD IQ is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at dovtekofficial@gmail.com and we will delete the data promptly.

Users between 13 and 18 should use the service only with the knowledge and consent of a parent or guardian.

09

International Users

SHIELD IQ is operated from Nigeria and is built in compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023. If you are accessing SHIELD IQ from outside Nigeria, your data may be transferred to and processed in Nigeria where data protection laws may differ from those in your country.

By using SHIELD IQ, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data in Nigeria in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

We are working toward compliance with additional international data protection frameworks including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Economic Area.

11

Legal Basis for Processing

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023, we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

Contract performance — processing your account data and scan metadata is necessary to provide the SHIELD IQ service you have signed up for.

Legitimate interests — we process usage and device data to maintain and improve the security and functionality of the service. Our legitimate interest in operating a safe and effective fraud detection service is balanced against your right to privacy.

Legal obligation — we may process data where required to comply with applicable law or legal process.

Consent — for any processing not covered by the above bases (such as marketing communications), we will seek your explicit consent and you may withdraw it at any time.
