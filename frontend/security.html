Security

Data Storage & Security

We take your privacy and security seriously. Here is how we ensure your data is protected at every step of the process.

01

Encryption Standards

Your account data and scan metadata are stored on secure servers. We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and encryption at rest for sensitive data.

Passwords are never stored in plain text. We use a strong, one-way hashing algorithm (bcrypt) that ensures even SHIELD IQ staff cannot read your password.

02

Memory-Only Processing

Message content is never stored: The text you paste or the images you upload for scanning are processed in memory only and are never written to disk or any persistent storage system at any point in our analysis pipeline.

03

Abuse Prevention

To maintain security and prevent abuse, we monitor usage data to detect unusual patterns that may indicate abuse of our free tier or attempts to misuse the service. This helps us keep the platform safe for everyone.

04

Data Breach Protocol

Despite our best efforts, no security system is absolutely impenetrable. In the highly unlikely event of a data breach that affects your personal data, we commit to notifying you within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, in strict accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023.
